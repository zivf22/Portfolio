Park
Hunt

Parking app

Problem

Finding a free parking space in a parking lot is not an easy task. It takes up valuable time, it creates unnecessary fights with people over parking spaces, it often hurts your pocket and then in the end it turns out that you parked too far away.

Solution

That's why I created PARKHUNT. No more frustrating parking experiences. With the click of a button you can find the closest and cheapest parking space. And that's not all — you can also see how many free parking spaces are left, and as soon as you enter the parking lot, you will be directed to the best available parking space designated especially for you.
