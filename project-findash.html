Parking app

Problem

Finding a free parking space is not an easy task. It takes up valuable time, creates unnecessary conflict, often hurts your pocket, and in the end you park too far away.

Solution

That's why I created PARKHUNT. No more frustrating parking experiences. With the click of a button you can find the closest and cheapest parking space.

You can also see how many free spaces are left, and as soon as you enter the parking lot, you will be directed to the best available spot designated especially for you.
